Directions to JNP Banquet Hall Naigaon

✿

Step-by-step directions from all major nearby localities to our banquet hall in Naigaon East.

5–10 mins

From Naigaon Railway Station

Exit Naigaon East platform → Take an auto-rickshaw / cab towards Don Bosco Road → JNP Banquet Hall is approx. 1.5 km from the station. Board Western Railway trains from CST, Dadar, Borivali, Vasai or Virar.

10–15 mins

From Vasai Road Station

Exit Vasai Road station (East) → Board a local train towards Virar / Dahanu → Get off at Naigaon station (next stop) → Auto-rickshaw to Don Bosco Road, Naigaon East.

10–20 mins

From Virar / Nalasopara

Board Western Railway slow local towards Mumbai CST/Churchgate → Get off at Naigaon station → Auto-rickshaw to JNP Banquet Hall, Don Bosco Road (5 mins).

15–25 mins

From Bhayandar / Mira Road (by Car)

Take NH48 (Mumbai–Ahmedabad Highway) northbound → Take Naigaon East exit → Follow Don Bosco Road signs → JNP Banquet Hall is clearly visible with ample roadside parking.

25–40 mins

From Borivali / Dahisar (by Car)

Take Western Express Highway or NH48 northbound via Mira Road → Naigaon East exit → Don Bosco Road. Alternatively, Western Railway train to Naigaon station (approx. 30 mins from Borivali).

40–60 mins

From Mumbai (Andheri / BKC)

Western Express Highway → NH48 north → Naigaon East exit → Don Bosco Road. Or Western Railway from Andheri / Dadar / CST → Naigaon Station (fast local ~45 mins) → Auto to hall.

40–60 mins

From Palghar / Boisar

Take NH48 southbound → Naigaon East exit → Don Bosco Road, Naigaon East. Or Western Railway from Palghar or Virar towards Mumbai → Naigaon station.

5 mins

Local Auto-Rickshaw from Naigaon

Auto-rickshaws are readily available at Naigaon East station and throughout Naigaon town. Just say "JNP Banquet Hall, Don Bosco Road" — all local auto drivers know the venue.

JNP Banquet Hall — Centrally Located in Naigaon East

JNP Banquet Hall is strategically situated in Naigaon East, Vasai-Virar, Maharashtra (PIN 401208) on Don Bosco Road — one of Naigaon's most accessible and well-connected streets. Our location offers the perfect balance: central enough for guests arriving from all directions, yet situated away from the congestion of South Mumbai. The hall is visible and accessible from the main road, with no confusing inner lanes or difficult approach routes.

The venue is equidistant from Vasai Road (one station south) and Nallasopara (one station north), making it the most centrally located premier banquet hall in Naigaon East for guests from across the Vasai-Virar Municipal Corporation area. With the Mumbai–Ahmedabad National Highway (NH48) just minutes away, guests driving in from Mira Road, Bhayandar, Borivali, Dahisar, and all of South and Central Mumbai can reach JNP conveniently by car or cab.

For guests using Mumbai's extensive Western Railway suburban network, Naigaon station is directly served by all slow local trains between CST/Churchgate and Virar/Palghar. The station-to-venue distance of just 1.5 km (5–10 minutes by auto) makes JNP the most train-accessible banquet hall in the Vasai-Virar region. Elderly guests, guests with children, and out-of-town families particularly appreciate the convenience of arriving by train with no long taxi journeys.

Our 150+ vehicle dedicated parking, valet assistance, and zero traffic-congestion approach route make JNP Banquet Hall not just the most beautiful, but also the most practically accessible banquet hall in Naigaon for events of all sizes. Whether you are hosting 50 guests for an intimate engagement or 300 guests for a grand wedding, our location ensures your event starts and ends on a positive note for every attendee.
