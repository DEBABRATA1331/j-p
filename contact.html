Frequently Asked Questions

✿
What is the maximum seating capacity of J&P Banquet?
+
J&P Banquet Hall can comfortably accommodate up to 2,000 guests for large Format events. For cocktail-style or standing events, the hall can host up to 2,500 guests. For smaller, more intimate gatherings we also have a smaller hall for up to 300 guests.
Is outside catering allowed at J&P Banquet?
+
We have an excellent in-house catering team that offers a wide variety of multi-cuisine menus. Outside catering is not permitted for hygiene and quality assurance reasons. However, you are welcome to bring your own celebration cake or specific traditional sweets for distribution.
How do I block or confirm a date for my event?
+
You can confirm your date by submitting the inquiry form above or calling us directly. A booking is confirmed upon payment of a 25% advance deposit. The remaining amount is due 7 days before the event date.
Is there parking available at the venue?
+
Yes! We have a dedicated multi-level parking facility that can accommodate over 400 vehicles. For large events we also offer valet parking service as an add-on. Additionally, there is street parking available in the nearby lanes.
Can I visit the hall before booking?
+
Absolutely! We encourage site visits so you can see the space and visualise your event. Please call us or send an inquiry to schedule a free site tour with our event manager. We are available Monday through Saturday, 10 AM to 6 PM.
Do you allow decorators from outside the hall?
+
Yes! You may bring your own decorator. We have a list of preferred partners who are familiar with our venue and can set up efficiently. Our team will provide them access for setup the day before the event. Please note that all decorations must be approved by our venue manager.
What is the cancellation and refund policy?
+
Cancellations made 30+ days before the event date will receive a 75% refund of the advance deposit. Cancellations between 15–29 days prior will receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 14 days of the event are non-refundable. Date rescheduling is allowed once, subject to availability.
Is the hall fully air-conditioned?
+
Yes, J&P Banquet Hall is fully centrally air-conditioned throughout all areas including the main hall, foyer, bridal rooms, and washrooms. We also have a full generator backup to ensure uninterrupted power during your event.
